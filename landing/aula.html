Redesigning Communication Between Teachers and Parents

The project explores how the communication platform Aula can be redesigned to improve collaboration and reflection between teachers and parents. The concept aims to reduce stress, information overload, and constant availability — helping users communicate with greater intention and understanding.

Thesis from ITU

Design Process

1

Framing & Research

Critically analyzed Aula and existing communication practices to identify issues like stress, tone, and availability.

2

User Insights

Interviewed teachers, parents, and experts to uncover challenges in expectations and digital communication.

3

Design concept

Created ideas promoting reflection and balanced dialogue through metaphors and mindful communication tools..

4

Prototype & Testing

Developed and tested a high-fidelity prototype based on slow technology principles to support thoughtful interactions.
